Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about how Awantrix partners with Spectrum, Verizon, Frontier, Comcast, and other carriers to streamline your connectivity projects.

Once you share your service addresses, bandwidth needs, and any special requirements, we immediately engage Spectrum and other relevant carriers on your behalf. We normalize all returned pricing, term options, and installation notes into a clean comparison, usually within 24 business hours for standard locations. For complex fiber builds or DIA/wavelength designs, we will set expectations up‑front and keep you updated as carriers engineer their options.

You maintain a direct relationship and contract with each carrier—Spectrum, Verizon, Frontier, Comcast, or others. Awantrix acts as your neutral advisor and project manager, coordinating quotes, design, and installation, while carriers continue to provide and support the services. This model gives you the best of both worlds: direct carrier support plus a single partner who understands the full picture across all of your locations and providers.

At minimum, we need service addresses, target bandwidth ranges, preferred term length, and any special requirements (e.g., DIA vs. shared, redundancy needs, cloud on‑ramps, or compliance constraints). If you have existing Spectrum, Verizon, Frontier, or Comcast bills, we can also use them to benchmark current rates and shape renewal strategies. The more context you provide, the better we can tailor both carrier selection and architecture.

Carriers often deliver technical notes that do not always make sense to facilities or low‑voltage teams. We translate those into clear, actionable install packages: demarcation location, handoff type, space/power requirements, and any cabling that must be in place before the field tech arrives. We can also coordinate directly with your cabling vendors to ensure every site is ready before turn‑up.

There is typically no additional cost to your organization for working with Awantrix. As a connectivity partner, we are compensated by carriers when services are ordered—similar to how you would work with a channel partner—but our role is to remain carrier‑agnostic and aligned with your long‑term interests. In select cases where custom consulting or on‑site work is required, we will outline any fees clearly and in advance.
